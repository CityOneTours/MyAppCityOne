ABOUT US

Established in Dubai in 2012, with branches in Hyderabad and Africa, City One is an award-winning Destination Management Company (DMC) delivering exceptional travel experiences backed by decades of industry expertise.

We specialize in:

  • ✈️ Visa assistance & air-ticketing
  • 🎯 Custom-designed holiday packages
  • 🏢 MICE services (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences & Exhibitions)
  • 👥 Group travel planning & coordination
  • 🐪 Desert safaris, wellness retreats & business travel

Why choose us

At City One Tourism & Travel, we go beyond just bookings — we deliver experiences.
Here’s why travelers and corporate clients around the world trust us:

Expertise & Trust

With 12+ years of experience, we are a trusted name in the travel industry, known for delivering reliable, high-quality service backed by multiple TripAdvisor awards.

Global Presence

Headquartered in Dubai, with branches in Hyderabad and Africa, we offer both local insights and global travel solutions tailored to diverse markets.

Complete Travel Solutions

From visa support, ticketing, and holiday planning to MICE and group travel, we provide end-to-end services under one roof.

Personalized Service

Every trip is custom-designed to suit your goals, lifestyle, and budget—with 24/7 support to ensure a smooth experience from start to finish.
