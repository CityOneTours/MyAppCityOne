Documents Required: Tourist

  1. Original passport and copy of passport (Valid for more than 3 months from date of exit from Schengen Area) (with at least 2 blank pages side by side)
  2. Application form. (We will do it For You)
  3. Copies of previous Schengen Visas.
  4. Two recent passport pictures with white background, not older than 6 months.
  5. UAE Residence Visa copy (Valid for more than 3 months from date of exit from Schengen Area)
  6. Copy of Emirates ID.
  7. Original Employer NOC stating designation, salary, date of joining, purpose and date of travel, financial sponsorship. Valid for 1 month from date of issue with Original sign and Original stamp on it.
  8. Company Trade License (If Business Owner)
  9. Hotel and air ticket reservations. (We will do it For you)
  10. Proof of valid Travel health insurance covered in Schengen Area for a minimum of Euro 30,000 medical expenses. (We will do it for You)
  11. Last 3 months personal bank statement Original printed from Bank and Original Stamped by the Bank.
  12. Confirmed Appointment for Document submission and Biometrics (We will book for you)
